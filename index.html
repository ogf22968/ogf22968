Places!

I love to travel! I have been to many places throughout the past nineteen years! From Africa, to China to Europe, my family loves to travel. Overall, My favorite place I have ever been has been Africa. There is simply no other place like it! One of the best parts about Africa is the culture. The people are friendly. The food is delicious and the scenery is breathtaking. In Africa, I stayed at The LeWa Wildlife Conservancy, which was one of the most amazing places I have ever been.

One of my favorite quotes from a guest at LeWa is Thank you so much for giving us memories we will never forget. The backdrop, the food, the service, the drives, the animals and the birds were all spectacular.
